Transport Options in Pune

  • Air: Pune has an international airport, providing domestic and international flights.
  • Railway: Pune Junction is a major railway station connecting Pune to various cities across India.
  • Metro: Pune Metro is a rapid transit system under construction, aimed at improving intra-city travel.
  • Auto: Auto-rickshaws are a popular mode of local transport in Pune, offering convenient short-distance travel.
  • PMPL (Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Limited): PMPL operates an extensive bus network, catering to public transport needs across Pune.
