Notable Historical Places in Pune

Shaniwarwada Fort

Built: 1732

Details: The seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire, Shaniwarwada is known for its impressive architecture and rich history. It is also associated with local legends and the famous ghost story of Narayan Rao.

Aga Khan Palace

Built: 1892

Details: Built by Sultan Muhammad Shah Aga Khan, this palace is significant for its association with Mahatma Gandhi and the Indian independence movement. It houses a museum and has beautiful gardens.

Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum

Details: This museum, housed in a 20th-century building, showcases a vast collection of artifacts, sculptures, and paintings, offering a glimpse into India's cultural heritage.

Pataleshwar Cave Temple

Built: 8th century

Details: A rock-cut temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, this temple is an excellent example of early Indian rock-cut architecture, featuring a serene and peaceful atmosphere.
