Welcome to Pune!

Pune is a vibrant city in Maharashtra, India, known for its rich history, educational institutions, and more. It is often referred to as the cultural capital of Maharashtra and the Oxford of the East.

Founded in the 8th century, Pune has a deep historical significance and has been a center for politics, culture, and education throughout its history.

Today, Pune is recognized for:

  • Educational Excellence: Pune houses many prestigious educational institutions, including the University of Pune (Savitribai Phule Pune University), Symbiosis International University, and many more.
  • Cultural Diversity: The city boasts a rich cultural heritage with a blend of Maharashtrian, Marathi, and cosmopolitan influences.
  • IT and Industrial Hub: Pune is a major hub for information technology, automotive manufacturing, and other industries, attracting professionals and businesses from around the world.
  • Historical Landmarks: Explore Pune's historical landmarks such as Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, Sinhagad Fort, and various ancient temples that reflect its rich history.
  • Modern Amenities: Pune offers excellent infrastructure with a network of public transport including buses, metro, auto-rickshaws, and local trains (suburban rail).
  • Food and Entertainment: Enjoy Pune's vibrant food scene with a variety of Maharashtrian cuisine and international flavors, along with bustling markets, shopping malls, and nightlife options.
