MIT World Peace University (MITWPU)

MIT World Peace University is a private university located in Pune, Maharashtra. It offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fields like engineering, management, arts, and more.

College of Engineering Pune (COEP)

COEP is an autonomous engineering institute affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University. It is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India and offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines of engineering and technology.
